Focus on a room

The Pool House - The jewel in the crown of Tregulland: a 12.5m freshwater indoor pool of pure filtered Cornish spring water drawn up through the granite by our own borehole. The water is kept at a steady 27℃, and is cleaned and purified by plant magic. 3 giant biomass filters remove all the nasties so there is no need for stinky harmful chlorine​.​

​I​f you want to relax after your swim, we have a 6 person steam room to help you cleanse and a glorious wood fired hot tub just outside for the more adventurous.

Truly a recipe for health and happiness
