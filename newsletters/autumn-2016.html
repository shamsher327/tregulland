Eat Local

With our state of the art kitchens​ ​you can rustle up a feast! If you book a delivery from any of the big supermarkets between 2-4pm the day you arrive​, our staff will unpack your shopping for you. We'd also recommend ​a​ visit ​to ​the magnificent ​Philip ​Warren​ in Launceston to buy locally produced meat with impeccable provenance (used by many top restaurants in London) and why not pop along to the fish stall at Widemouth Bay​ for some of the most reasonably priced, freshly caught lobsters in Cornwall? They'll even cook them to order for you. If that's not enough, make your surf & turf sing with a starter from either of the award winning local businesses: Deli Farm Charcuterie, or Tregida Seafood - you'll be spoilt for choice!
​Autumn Glory

After all that delicious locally produced food, you'll want to walk it off in the beautiful Cornish countryside, the trees are ​just ​starting to change to their Autumn colours, blackberries are everywhere and the sloes are starting to ripen. We supply Ordnance Survey maps of the area in both houses, or look in the ​Guest Information for some of our favourite walks... If the weather is against you a few laps of the ​​Indoor Pool should suffice, and then you can curl up by the fire with a cuppa.
Autumn Special Offer

There are still a few weekend, week-long and midweek dates available this Autumn, quote AN16 when booking the whole property ​from now to Dec 18th 2016 and on arrival you'll be able to tuck into a luxury mixed hamper of locally produced goodies as a thank you from us!
Focus on a Room - The Barn Granary

The Granary sitting room in the Barn is a huge room with its original vaulted ceiling and Philippe Starck sofa, a large card table and a selection of traditional games to play. Stoke up the enormous woodburner, wrap yourselves in woolly blankets and make a nest in the squashy hessian grainsack beanbags to watch a film on the drop down cinema screen.
