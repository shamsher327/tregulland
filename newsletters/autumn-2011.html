Nature Watch

As Bodmin Moor grows cooler (heatwave aside!) we've been noticing the seasonal changes around the grounds. Over the weekend we spotted two beautiful  Tawny owls hanging out on some stones. They are being very vocal right now as the youngsters are establishing their winter territories so are expecting to see a lot more of them. In fact we're planning to install an owlcam so visitors to the website can see what they're up to throughout the year..

Splish Splash Splosh

Finally, we wanted to share the story of our magnificent Swimming Pool, the first indoor freshwater pool in Britain designed by Clearwater Revival, and which is hugely decadent. Freshwater pools are chemical free, using the Rolls Royce of plant filtration systems to remove bacteria and maintain the water in an absolutely clean state. It's like wild swimming but indoors and as well as a main 12x5 metre pool, there's a kids pool, shower, steam room with aromatic vapours, and an outdoor Dutch Tub which is great fired up in the evening, under the stars..
