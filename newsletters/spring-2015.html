Nature
Most people reading this will wonder why we have included a photo of what looks like a poo on the patio! In fact it is a pellet from a Barn owl. Owl pellets are fascinating objects that are made up of the indigestible remains of everything the owl has eaten: usually the regurgitated fur and bones of voles, mice and shrews. The fact that it has left us a pellet means he or she has checked out our nest box in the end of the Barn and we hope they will choose to nest there soon.
