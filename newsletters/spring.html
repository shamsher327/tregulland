Families and children at Tregulland

 

We have been pleased to welcome groups with lots of children who have made good use of the play area and table tennis but the activity to steal the show is our Bushcraft Skills Workshop run by Jay Parsons and The Outdoor Club. This is an exciting addition to our bespoke activities and includes, for example,  shelter building, fire making, animal tracking and music sessions.  Please contact Bonny to book or for further information on any of our bespoke activities.

Kick back and relax while the kids are busy…

 

While the kids are busy we are pleased to be able to offer a range of spa treatments and specialist sports massage by our qualified therapists. 

Enjoy the indoor freshwater pool, hot tub and steam room while the kids are in the grounds having fun. Then relax with a hot stone massage in the comfort of your own room or poolside! And when all the hard work is done, throw open the doors to the sunroom with a glass of Camel Valley.

Food

 

Our catering continues to be successful, The Seafood Restaurant tasting menu offers guests and exclusive tasting menu for those special occasions. We are are pleased to announce a new partnership with Cornish Delicacies brining the best local food to your door at Tregulland. We can also arrange barbeques, breakfasts or afternoon tea. Please contact Bonny for further information or sample menus.  

Nature at Tregulland

 

A busy time of year we can see everything bursting into life before our eyes. A charm of Goldfinches will welcome you at the top of the lane at this time of year, one after the other will dance over the hedges following the car. Not long before the lily pads on the lake burst into life which will give our 8 goslings something to play with! We've even got a mallard duck nest on the banks of the lake, we look forward to welcoming the new family to the lake.

Check our nature page for more info or get updates by following us on Twitter.
