​The Disappearing Stream

Some of our keenest Insta followers and sharp-eyed Summer guests have wondered why we have a series of dry steps beside the cottage at Tregulland. Its only in the depths of winter (like now) that you see what's going on: Tregulland's driveway was built over a runoff stream, so although it is dry throughout the summer, after the usual amount of winter rainfall (like now) we see the little stream trickling merrily down the special steps we made for it. So, that explains the mystery of the disappearing stream, and we've found it's excellent for cork boat races!
