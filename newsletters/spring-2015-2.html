Nature.
Spring is really settling in now, the first swallows are clearly overjoyed to be back, the Canada geese are getting ready to hatch their eggs on the island in our lake and everything is bursting with life. We have a nature treasure trail all set up for little ones to help you explore the flora and fauna in our perfectly proportioned lake, woodland, grounds and rivers.
