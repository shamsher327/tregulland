Cycling

 

Recently guests have been enjoying days out on their bikes, we even had a tandem! The Camel Trail provides some lovely rides, long and short and as it's along the old railway line, there aren't the hills we associate with Cornwall. See Cornwall Council website for more Camel Trail Info and contact Bridge Bike Hire in Wadebridge or Padstow Cycle Hire in Padstow. We like to cycle from Wadebridge, stop for Fish and Chips at Rick Stein's before heading back!

Indoor Pool, Hot Tub, Steam Room...

 

Probably BECAUSE of beautiful Cornwall's unpredictable weather, our indoor pool, steam room and fabulous outdoor hot tub are proving to be an added draw for many of our guests. What could be better than sipping a chilled glass of bubbly in a cosily warm hot tub as the elements swirl around you? Or take a refreshing dip in our spring-fed, freshwater pool (no chlorine), before letting your muscles totally relax in the aromatherapy steam room - utter bliss!

Nature at Tregulland

 

Blue skies and sun have finally taken over from the early morning drizzle. Our summer swallows look happy soaring over the lake at Tregulland.

Chiffchaffs are amongst the many birds heard calling from the woodland and skylarks sing high above the fields around the neighbouring farm. Everthing has burst into life over the last couple of weeks and as the temperature increases we can see splashes of colour as the wild flowers blossom.

Check our nature page for more info or get updates by following us on Twitter.
