Focus ​on a​ ​R​oom - Celandine​
At the top of its own staircase, Celandine​ has an elegant French country house feel with a superking size bed in a room flooded with light. From the metal birdcages, vintage tapestry cushions and wheatsheaf chandeliers this room is the epitome of country charm. Take the weight off your feet in our cosy corner chair to read awhile, sit at the desk as you look out over the gorgeous landscaped fishing lake with Bodmin moor stretching beyond or why not both sit back and relax in the double ended silver rolltop bath and soak all your cares away with our delicious Ren bath oil? Then slide between our crisp white Baumont&Brown 100% cotton, 400 thread count bedding with feather duvets and piles of sumptuous down surround pillows....You'll never want to leave.
