For escaping, exploring, entertaining and dreaming
Hello, its been an incredible Summer here at Tregulland, helped by some brilliant sunny weather! The Met Office says this should make for a spectacular Autumn. As Cornwall proves itself as an all year round destination, why not make the most of it at Tregulland? Enjoy the changing seasons with some great food, an Autumn walk and then settle down in front of one of our log fires with some mulled cider or a hot chocolate? If the weather really draws in you can always warm up in the sauna and take a dip in our indoor freshwater pool.
Prices & Availability
Our peak weeks usually get snapped up well in advance. However, we do still have a week in the Barn from 20th December which means you will be celebrating Christmas with us. As with every year the houses will be decorated and the Barn is not complete without a huge 16 foot tree! We are also offering 10% off bookings before the end of March and we’re even throwing in a luscious giant cupcake for you to feast on.

Things to do - The Firepit
Enjoy the warmth and glow of an open fire. Ideal for summer and winter evenings. Tregulland's two properties are ideal taken together for large group holidays and celebrations of up to 22+ people. The Barn sleeps ten people while the Cottage sleeps twelve.
Things to do while you stay at Tregulland
Drumbecue
For guests who love food, or simply want to forget about cooking for a while, Tregulland's food suppliers use local, seasonal and organic produce where possible to create delicious and varied menus.

Sunroom
With full length doors on 3 sides, The Sun Room looks right onto the valley, and offers the perfect spot for some relaxation or a doze. In the summer open up the sides and spill out onto the lawn. Or if its raining settle down with a mug of hot chocolate, enjoy the views and watch the weather roll up the valley.
