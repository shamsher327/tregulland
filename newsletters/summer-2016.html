Nature Update
Tregulland is Golden!

With all the sunshine and rain recently Tregulland is Blooming Marvellous!

We have huge King Cups on the lake, exuberant Buttercups in the field and giant clumps of Cowslips dotting the land with cheery yellow flowers. The Gorse is particularly happy and as the saying goes: “Kissing’s out of fashion when the Gorse is NOT in bloom” (ie never). Gorse smells amazingly tropical in the sunshine; of coconuts and pineapples and apparently you can eat the flowers too! Sunshiny-beautiful everywhere you look.
