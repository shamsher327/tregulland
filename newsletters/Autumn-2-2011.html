Autumn Newsletter 2011
www.tregulland.co.uk

Luxurious self-catering holiday home in Cornwall

For escaping, exploring, entertaining and dreaming

Dear Friends,

Some of you may have heard about one of the projects my wife Illona and I have had on the go for a while, which I'm delighted to say has finally come to life. Tregulland is a holiday home which has just opened after years of renovation and if you haven't heard about it, I'll explain...

For some reason I had the madcap idea a few years ago of buying a derelict barn and cottage in 16 acres of beautiful grounds just by Bodmin Moor in Cornwall. The plan was to create a lovely new holiday pad for large groups, great for all ages, and designed to be completely sustainable and energy efficient. I didn't want to do things by half so basically went all out.

Fast forward a couple of years, we've not reopened after a top-to-toe refurbishment as a holiday home sleeping 22 people and with a range of bespoke guest services, from private dinners to surf school, massages and yoga. Some of you have been directly involved or may have heard me banging on about it recently.

Anyway, we are now open for business and I'd love you to have a look at the website and let me know what you think. Get in touch if you're planning any breaks and think this might be the ticket, and I'd be grateful if you'd pass the link on to any friends who would love the Tregulland vibe.

There are a few things that really are extra special: Tregulland has the first indoor freshwater swimming pool in the UK which is completely ecological and results in pure water that's out of this world. We have an HD cinema projection screen. The whole place is heated using cutting-edge sustainable technology. There's a 1 acre fishing lake for all you anglers, and we're really proud of our interiors which were put together by Marcus Crane.

As it's our first year, we're offering a discount of 25% off any booking made before December 25th, for holidays taken before the end of June 2012. We're pretty booked up around Christmas, but we do have availability in October half-term week for whoever's first past the post, at a juicy 35% off for a lastminute deal. Our booking system is online here.

Very much hope you like what we've done. Have a great  autumn, and maybe see you at Tregulland!

Best Wishes

Ken and Illona Aylmer
